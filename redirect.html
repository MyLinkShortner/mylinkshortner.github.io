⏱️ The Science Behind the 20‑second Wait

Many users ask: “Why 20 seconds?” It’s a balance between security efficacy and user convenience. Our internal research shows that 20 seconds is the sweet spot to deter automated scraping bots, which account for nearly 40% of all link preview requests. Bots typically timeout after 5–10 seconds, making our gateway an effective barrier. Additionally, this window allows our heuristic engines to cross‑reference the destination IP against multiple threat intelligence feeds (AlienVault OTX, Abuse.ch, etc.) without impacting the user experience.

During this interval, the page also performs a silent integrity check of the local environment: verifying that the browser is not running in a headless mode commonly used by malicious scripts. While no personal data is ever collected, we generate a one‑time session token to prevent replay attacks. The timer also provides a natural moment for human visitors to read about our privacy commitments — we believe transparency is key. Moreover, the countdown creates a psychological “safety buffer,” reassuring users that the destination has been vetted. As soon as the timer reaches zero, the button activates and the verified URL is ready with a single click.
